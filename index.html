The Cookie Monster

Who am I

I am a voracious monster. I am covered with blue fur, I have a pair of googly eyes, and I have an insatiable appetite. As my name implies, my primary craving is cookies, but I can (and often do) consume anything and everything, from apples and pie to letters, flatware, and hubcaps. When I eats something, I makes a very distinct, loud munching "noise", often interpreted as "OMM-nom-nom-nom..."
